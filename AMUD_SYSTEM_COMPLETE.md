## Design Philosophy

### Borrowed from Your Guide System

Just like your Daily Living Guides have:
- **Beginner** - Simple, encouraging, practical
- **Intermediate** - Traditional methods
- **Advanced** - Complete halachic detail

The Amud System has:
- **Learn** - Understand the basics
- **Quick** - See the path
- **Full** - Know the scope
- **Rehearsal** - Practice the words

### Key Principles

1. **Progressive disclosure** - Start simple, add complexity as needed
2. **User choice** - Let them pick their level
3. **Complete transparency** - No hidden details at higher levels
4. **Calendar-aware** - Automatically adjust for today
5. **Encouraging tone** - "You've got this!" not "You'll mess up"
6. **Visual hierarchy** - Icons, colors, borders for quick scanning
7. **Practical focus** - What you'll DO, not just what you should know
